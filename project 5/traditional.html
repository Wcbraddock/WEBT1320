What is Traditional Animation

Traditional Animation is the classic hand drawn animation. This was the first animation medium to ever be used, popularized mostly by iconic disney films. Each drawing would be slightly different from the last in hopes of creating the illusion of motion. These animations started out being synced with music or audio in realtime.
