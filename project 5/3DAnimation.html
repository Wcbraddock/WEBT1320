What is 3D Animation

3D Animation is similar to 2d Rigged animation. It uses a virual skeleton on a 3D figure called a Mesh, or Model. But what makes it different is the adition of the Z axis. As well as the ability to easily change the angle of the animation without having to painfully redraw everything.
