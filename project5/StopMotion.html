What is Stop Motion?

Stop Motion is animation of figures captured at one frame at a time. The figures are moved in between frames to create the illusion of fluid motion when played back. Much similar to that of 2D animation. (see Traditional Animation) To give a visual example, check the video of the 2 planes below.
