About Pages and Tabs

Pages and Tabs was founded by Weston braddock while learning coding languages. He then took it a step further and started a commission to make a down payment on a house. It wasn't until a surge of requests for a website started in 2022 that began to overwhelm him. So he started hiring help and continued until he was able to balance the workload. From that moment on, Weston grew his team into the company today. We now function like a well oiled machine in the digital age.
